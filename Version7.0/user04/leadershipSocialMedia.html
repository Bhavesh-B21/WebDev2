Leaders

Aryan Padmanabhan

Aryan has been the lead trader and main admin of The Option Guys since the summer of 2020 and has changed the structure of the community from a paid discord into a free trading community where everyone makes the trades together by talking about different stocks, bonds, and options and allows for the growth of all the members while teaching them. While others have joined and become admin since or left the group and stopped acting as admin, Aryan has been the longest standing admin in the group and thus, is the most active and helpful resource by far.

The Option Guys are not just a free flowing community, we have a couple of traders that provide callouts of trades to help some of the newer members and are excellent learning resources for the rest of the group. We are a very non-hierarchical community, with no leaders towering over the members; however, we do have different leaders capable of different tasks and abilities in the discord which is all purely based on trading experience and experience of being in the discord. Aryan is one such member with a standing as one of the longest reigning members as well as being able to provide accurate callouts allowing him to run the discord effectively and positively.
